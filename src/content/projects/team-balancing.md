A web app that takes the hassle out of splitting players into fair teams for casual football matches.

Add players manually or import from Excel, assign categories (goalkeeper, defender, etc.), and generate balanced rosters in seconds. Not happy with the result? Hit randomize for a new configuration.

Built for local football teams that want to enjoy competitive games.
