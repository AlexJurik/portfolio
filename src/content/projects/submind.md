A subscription tracker built to help you stop losing money on forgotten renewals.

Submind keeps manual subscriptions and imported invoice-based subscriptions in one dashboard, shows monthly and annual spend totals, and sends reminder emails before the next billing date.

## Features

- **Renewal Reminders**: Get email reminders ahead of upcoming billing dates so subscriptions do not slip by unnoticed.
- **Spend Overview**: Review active subscriptions alongside monthly and annual totals from one overview screen.
- **Manual and Imported Tracking**: Add subscriptions yourself or import new invoice emails from Gmail after connecting your inbox.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: React
- **Backend**: Convex
- **Email Import**: Gmail invoice detection for recurring subscriptions
- **Localization**: English and Slovak interface support
